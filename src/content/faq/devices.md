question: "What devices are compatible with your service?"
category: "general"
order: 2
---

Our service works on a wide range of devices including Smart TVs (Samsung, LG, Sony, Android TV), streaming devices (Amazon Fire Stick, Roku, Apple TV, Chromecast), mobile devices (iOS and Android phones/tablets), and computers (Windows, Mac, Linux). You can also use IPTV apps like TiviMate, IPTV Smarters, or our custom app available on all major platforms.
