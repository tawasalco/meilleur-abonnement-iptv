question: "How do I set up the IPTV service on my device?"
category: "setup"
order: 1
---

Setting up our IPTV service is quick and easy! After subscribing, you'll receive an email with your login credentials and a simple step-by-step guide. We support most devices including Smart TVs (Samsung, LG, Android TV), Amazon Fire Stick, iOS and Android devices, and Windows/Mac computers. Our support team is available 24/7 to help if you need any assistance during setup.
