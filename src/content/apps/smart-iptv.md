Simple IPTV player designed specifically for Smart TVs.

## Why Choose Smart IPTV?

Smart IPTV is one of the best IPTV players that works perfectly with meilleur abonnement iptv. It offers:

- Easy M3U and Xtream Codes support
- Electronic Program Guide (EPG) integration
- Favorites and playlist management
- Regular updates and improvements
- Clean, user-friendly interface

## Supported Platforms

Smart IPTV is available on: Samsung TV, LG TV, Android TV

## How to Setup with meilleur abonnement iptv

1. Download Smart IPTV from your device's app store
2. Open the app and go to settings or add playlist
3. Choose your preferred method (M3U URL or Xtream Codes API)
4. Enter the credentials provided after your meilleur abonnement iptv subscription
5. Wait for channels to load and start watching

## Tips for Best Experience

- Use a stable internet connection (minimum 10 Mbps for HD, 25 Mbps for 4K)
- Enable EPG for a better TV guide experience
- Create favorites lists for quick access to your preferred channels
- Keep the app updated for latest features and improvements
